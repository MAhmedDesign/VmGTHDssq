About Me

Hi! My name is Mohammad Zayn Ahmed and I'm an aspiring technology enthusiast in Polk County, Florida. I dabble in many fields of graphic design and associated skills for fun and professionally. When I was in fifth grade my mother let me handle a camera. Just a few years later I would branch off into the editing sphere at my school's computer lab, which opened up a whole new world to me. It allowed me to combine the skills I already knew with new ones I discovered to explore the advances in modern technology.

Since then, I've been honing my graphic design, video editing, and more. I'd say I'm passionate about using what tools I have to apply creative solutions and look forward to whatever comes my way next. When it comes to multimedia projects, I'm always up for the challenge.
